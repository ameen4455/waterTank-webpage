About our Organisation

Megha Water Tanks is a prominent name among plastic water store tank manufacturers in South India. It is a premium product of Cochin Polymers Pvt Ltd. The name Cochin Polymers Pvt Ltd is written in golden letters in the history of rotational molding industry in Kerala with the beginning of its first facility in 1993. The company was ventured as a small scale industrial unit at Kalamassery, Ernakulam District. Our business spans around manufacturing, purchasing, selling, exporting and importing of plastic goods, including plastic tanks and containers.

Megha Water Tanks stands atop its competitors as the most trusted brand since 25 years. Our steadfast quality built on our history of excellence and experience makes us the most preferred choice across South India. Megha pursue a well-defined quality management system that ensures top-grade raw materials and high engineering standards to deliver premium quality products. We offer a wide array of water tanks to meet the varied requirements of customers.

Our exclusive range of water tanks has the following features:

• Manufactured from polyethylene having excellent Environmental Stress Crack Resistance (ESCR), high load bearing properties and non-corrosive materials capable of withstanding adverse weather conditions
• Appropriate stabilizer additives to provide high UV resistance and similar atmospheric hazards
• Made from FDA approved food grade polyethylene
• Clean, smooth, seamless and warpless inner and outer walls
• Molded in single piece without any joints or patches making them durable and leak proof
• Non-toxic and non-absorbing water tanks without any color or taste
• Easy to clean and maintain
• Available in assorted shapes and capacities ranging from 200 liters to 5,000 liters
• Competitive pricing
